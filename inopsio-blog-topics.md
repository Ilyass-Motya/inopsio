## Content Strategy Tips for Maximum LinkedIn Engagement:

### Headlines That Hook:
- Use shocking statistics (78%, 99%, $1.85 trillion)
- Create urgency with time-sensitive phrases ("2025", "Coming Crisis")
- Include power words ("Nightmare", "Apocalypse", "Reality Check")
- Promise insider knowledge ("Exposed", "What They Don't Tell You")

### Structure for LinkedIn:
1. **Hook:** Start with a shocking statistic or provocative statement
2. **Problem:** Agitate the pain point with real examples
3. **Solution:** Position Inopsio as the answer (without being too salesy)
4. **Call to Action:** Invite discussion, comments, or DMs for more info

### Engagement Boosters:
- Include 3-5 relevant hashtags (#AI #Cybersecurity #Manufacturing #Enterprise #DigitalTransformation)
- Tag industry thought leaders in comments
- Ask controversial questions to spark debate
- Share exclusive insights or "insider information"
- Use data-driven content with specific numbers and sources

### Content Calendar Suggestion:
- Post 2-3 times per week
- Mix industry trends (60%) with Inopsio-specific content (40%)
- Engage actively in comments within first 2 hours
- Cross-promote on other channels
- Create follow-up posts based on highest engagement topics
